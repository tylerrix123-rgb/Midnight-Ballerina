The Framework

Two pillars

Run one or stack both. Most performers cycle Lean and Glow together — the compounds are complementary, not competing.

01

Lean

The main stage pillar. Retatrutide 5mg — triple-receptor agonist for body composition research. Run as an 8–12 week protocol; once-weekly subcutaneous administration. Pairs cleanly with the Glow pillar — no pathway conflicts.

02

Glow & Skin

The camera pillar. Melanotan-2 nasal spray — pre-mixed, no needles, no reconstitution — for pigmentation pathway research. GHK-Cu Glow Blend for collagen research. Glutathione for cellular clarity protocols. Stage lighting hides nothing — this is the pillar that handles HD.

Sample Research Day

A night in protocol

A research timeline mapped to a working performer's day. Educational reference only — actual research subject schedules will vary by study design.

2:00 PM
Wake · Hydrate · Reconstitute
Subject rises. Bacteriostatic water reconstitution check. Hydration logged. No injections this window — fasted state preferred for Retatrutide later.
3:30 PM
Lean Window
Retatrutide subcutaneous administration. Once-weekly dosing — same day, same approximate time. Track injection site rotation. Do not stack with Glow compounds in the same window.
5:00 PM
Glow Window
GHK-Cu Glow Blend and Glutathione administered. Melanotan-2 nasal spray — 2 sprays per nostril — run on alternating days, no reconstitution required. Skin-pillar compounds run separately from Lean window for cleaner data isolation.
9:00 PM
Stage Hours
Working hours. No injections during active window. Hydration, electrolytes, and tracked food intake only.
4:00 AM
Recovery Window
BPC-157 / TB-500 (Encore) subcutaneous administration before sleep — dual-compound soft-tissue and systemic repair pathways. For performers with high physical output.
5:00 AM
Sleep Phase
Targeted 7–9 hours. Sleep is when the Glow and Lean protocols do the bulk of their work. Protect this window.
Stacking Theory

How bundles are built

Every Midnight Ballerinas bundle follows three rules. We don't just throw compounds in a box.

i

Complementary, not redundant

Each compound in a bundle covers a different pathway. The Glow Stack pairs a pigmentation compound with a collagen peptide and a cellular antioxidant — three distinct mechanisms, one visual result.

ii

Dose-windowed

Bundles are sized for an 8–12 week research framework. Quantities aren't arbitrary — they're matched to the cycle length we recommend for the underlying compound class.

iii

Reconstitution-ready

Every bundle ships with bacteriostatic water sized for the contents. No second order required. No guesswork on dilution ratios — our protocol card walks you through every vial.

iv

Cycle-aware

Advanced bundles include cycle support so the protocol off-ramp is as clean as the on-ramp. Research shows the descent matters as much as the climb.
